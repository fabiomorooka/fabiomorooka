Professional experiences

Software Developer at Stone Pagamentos | 10/2020 - Currently

As a software developer in the Accessory Obligations team within the Controllership Tech Team, I was responsible for developing the applications that generate the accessory obligations that the company must provide to the Brazilian government and its federal units (states). In addition, I developed technologies to generate brand's reports (full acquirer and van brands). The applications were developed in .NET and Python, using MySQL databases.

Key words: .NET, Python, MySQL, Accessory Obligations, Controllership.

Internship at Atos Integration | 03/2020 - 09/2020

I was an intern at Atos Integration in Rennes. I developed a natural language processing chatbot. The objective was to develop a friendly user chatbot with the objective to manage and facilitate the use of a continuous integration/continuous delivery chain. The project was developed in Python with the RASA framework, and was deployed in a Docker infrastructure orchestrated by Kubernetes and deployed by Ansible.

Key words: NLP, Chatbot, DevOps, Docker, Kubernetes, Ansible, CI/CD chain.

Internship at Centrale Supélec | 05/2019 - 09/2019

I was an intern at the Signals and Systems Laboratory (L2S) of Centrale Supélec in France. During this internship, I first studied deconvolution algorithms and then, developed an algorithm for the SKA France radio astronomy project. In addition, I had the opportunity to further study parallelism in a cluster infrastructure, and then to analyze the behavior of algorithms in this infrastructure.

Key words: Python, DASK framework, parallelism, HPC, deconvolution.

Risk Analyst at Stone Pagamentos | 10/2017 - 07/2018

I was a risk analyst at Stone Pagamentos in the Chargeback team. I was responsible for supporting the chargeback team by developing tools and automating processes. In addition, I had the opportunity to learn more about Chargeback in the payments market by handling certain chargeback cases. The applications were developed in .NET, Python and VBA using MySQL databases.

Key words: Python, VBA, .NET, MySQL, Chargeback, Risk.
